Personal Reflection


Experience in the Library Profession

My experience in the library world has been very fulfilling as I have gotten to learn how important library spaces are for the development of society and how they have changed drastically over time. I have been fortunate enough to work in public libraries that promote professional growth and various learning opportunities across departments. My study experience in School of Information Science & Learning Technologies has been short, but I have learned that there is a balance that is needed between my studies, my full-time position, and my personal life. It has been incredible seeing how my program encourages students to use real scenarios and apply them to our studies. Although I am still in the foundational stage of my degree, I look forward to the things I will learn that be added to my tool belt.


What have I learned

Although my time has been short, I have already learned a great amount. Through my Foundations of Library Science course, I was able to better understand the standards that are expected from libraries all over. I have been able to connect with library professionals outside of my area and have learned how real events impact our profession in immeasurable ways. In my Intro to Technologies course, I learned a great deal about the impact of developing technologies and its effect on how libraries have transformed over time. Through my program I hope to learn new skills that I may directly apply to my role at my public library.


How I have improved

While I have started to learn a more structured side of library professions, I have noticed that the courses I have taken have already benefited me in understanding the value of my profession. Knowing and upholding the fundamental values and ethics of librarianship has helped me navigate through real challenges that I am facing in the workplace. At a time where public library spaces are being tested by censorship, it has been incredibly helpful to rely on what I have learned to support my role. I have also learned to reach for help from others within my profession, as they go through similar experiences.


Goals for the future

As someone who holds a librarian title, a big goal for me is to complete my degree and feel confident in my current role. I look forward to learning about other areas within the public and private sphere that may spark new interest. As someone who has a passion for art and design, a goal of mine is to explore collection and archival avenues within library spaces. I have enjoyed my time in ISLT, and I hope that getting my degree will allow me to serve my community even more.
